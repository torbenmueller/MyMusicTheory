About this project

My name is Torben Jan M端ller, I am a freelance film composer. I studied at the Akademie Deutsche Pop in Cologne and graduated as a film composer, composer, arranger and artistic producer.

I have read many books about harmony and composition and have studied music theory intensively. What was always missing for me was a complete work where you can learn and look up everything that is important for musicians. You need a lot of different learning

sources and have to laboriously put everything together yourself. In addition, much of the content is too theoretical and has no connection to practice.

On the other hand, important topics such as the Sentence Form or the Period Form are not even mentioned. On this website I will gradually provide all the important topics that are relevant for composers.

The focus is on the benefits for writing music. Why do I need certain techniques and how do I use them? It covers basics, such as a checklist for composing a melody, as well as special topics such as modal interchange, practically applicable harmony, various composition techniques and much more.
